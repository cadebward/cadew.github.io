Old Farm Apartments

This site was designed by Josh Kirk. He simply handed me the fireworks file and asked me if I could do it. I was intimidated by his design, but I took the job anyways. I was surprised how quickly I was able to implement the features that scared me at first.

Complete Date: December 2013
See it Live

It was important for this website to be mobile ready. Plenty of students come looking for housing with nothing more than a phone to browse the internet with. I made sure to implement plenty of media queries.

I had a lot of fun building this website. The "old farm" feel definitely made for a style of websites I had never coded before.
